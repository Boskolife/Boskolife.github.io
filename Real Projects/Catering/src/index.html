Introducing our Chef

Max Tvorogov

The culinary mastermind responsible for some of the most delectable and diverse dishes you've ever tasted. Max has taken the culinary world by storm with his unique fusion of French, Italian, Slavic, and Asian flavors.

Childhood

Raised by a family of Slavic chefs who were fiercely proud, he was destined for greatness in the culinary arts. While his siblings played outside, he spent his youth mastering the art of chopping onions and garlic like a professional.

Culinary journey

Max was not satisfied with traditional Slavic cuisine; he aspired to push the limits of flavor and create something exceptional. Thus, he embarked on a culinary journey worldwide, training under the tutelage of the best chefs in France, Italy, and Asia.

The Art of Gastronomy

He is now a sensation in the culinary world, attracting crowds from all over the globe. His dishes are an inventive combination of Slavic spices, Cote d'Azur freshness, and a touch of Middle Eastern and Asian influence.

culinary adventure

If you're in the mood for a spicy falafel wrap or a rich and savory borscht, Max has got you covered. So if you're looking for a culinary adventure, remember his name!

Max Tvorogov
