how it works

How does the delivery work?

Each of your employees can choose their individual daily menu from our chef's pre-selected options. We will then deliver fresh lunches directly from our kitchen to your office every morning, neatly packed with utensils and all the necessary items. Because we value you, your time, your health, and the environment.

How soon will I receive my first order?

Our couriers work for you and deliver orders Monday through Friday from 9 am to 12 pm, depending on your zip code. The menu and orders are formed every week starting from Monday. So, make sure to place your order by the end of the week, Friday evening latest, to start receiving our delicious and healthy daily lunches on the upcoming Monday.

Is your food delivered fresh or frozen?

Only fresh! Our chefs prepare meals every day from carefully selected fresh ingredients, and our couriers deliver the containers in special thermos bags to ensure the quality of our lunches. judgment awards we enforce. As our clients, we work with the Judgment Creditors, as partners, to resolve the outstanding judgment debt.

Can I order your lunches if I have a food allergy or intolerance?

Yes, our menu is like a "constructor," with different options suitable for, for example, vegetarians or people with gluten intolerance, as well as for those who want to lose or gain weight. Please pay attention to the special markings!
