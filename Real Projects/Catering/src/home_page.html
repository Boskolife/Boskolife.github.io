About us

Date is a catering company that specializes in creating unique dining experiences for your corporate meals. The company is known for its exceptional food and drink offerings, which are crafted by the talented Chef Max Tvorogov and Mixologist Ivan Priemkin.

Chef Max Tvorogov is a highly skilled culinary artist who has trained in some of the best kitchens around the world. He has a passion for using fresh, seasonal ingredients to create dishes that are both delicious and visually stunning. Chef Max has developed a signature style that blends classic techniques with modern twists, resulting in dishes that are both timeless and innovative.

Mixologist Ivan Priemkin is an expert in crafting cocktails and creating unique drink experiences. He has a deep knowledge of spirits and ingredients, and he uses this expertise to create drinks that are not only delicious but also visually stunning. Ivan is passionate about creating drinks that are customized to the occasion, ensuring that every event has its unique signature drink.
