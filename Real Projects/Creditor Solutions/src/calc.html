Judgment Calculator

Most judgments awarded in New York State before April 30, 2022 accrue interest at 9% per year, or 0.75 per month. On the other hand, judgments awarded after April 30, 2022 accrue interest at 2% per year, or 0.167 percent per month. This calculator will help you estimate the total value and amount of interest earned on a court-awarded judgment in the state of New York. To start, enter the amount of the original judgment award.
