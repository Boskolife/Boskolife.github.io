Facebook

However, if you’re new to Facebook advertising, you might be wondering how to set up targeted advertising on the platform.

In this article, we’ll walk you through the steps involved in setting up targeted advertising on Facebook, so you can reach the right audience with your ads and achieve your marketing objectives.

Audience Insights

This tool allows you to explore data about Facebook users in general, including their demographics, interests, and behavior. You can use this data to create a target audience that is relevant to your business.

Facebook Pixel

This tool allows you to track the behavior of users on your website, including the pages they visit and the actions they take. You can use this data to create a target audience that is more likely to be interested in your products or services. Alternatively, you can create your own custom audience using your existing customer data. This could be your email list, website visitors, or people who have engaged with your brand on social media.
