What Are SEO Services?

SEO services refer to the offerings of an agency, freelancer, or consultant aimed at enhancing your website's SEO. Such services are designed to boost your web traffic and revenue through organic searches. Typically, SEO packages cover on-page, off-page, and technical aspects of SEO. Some service providers, however, might have a more concentrated focus, such as off-page SEO.

Types of SEO Services

Although conventional SEO plans satisfy most organizations' requirements, local or e-commerce businesses might gain an edge with local and e-commerce SEO strategies to maximize their SEO strategy's effectiveness. 

SEO services could focus on specific aspects of SEO, encompassing:

  • On-page SEO, emphasizing optimizations related to your website like title tags, content, and others.
  • Off-page SEO, prioritizing optimizations connected to your online presence, such as your website's backlinks.
  • Technical SEO, underscoring optimizations linked to your website's back end, including page speed, site architecture, and more.

As successful SEO entails on-page, off-page, and technical SEO, it's rare for businesses to invest in individual services. More often, companies select full-service SEO plans that incorporate on-page, off-page, and technical SEO as these packages offer an all-rounded solution to SEO needs.  

Keyword research

Keyword research service refers to a professional service that helps businesses identify relevant keywords to optimize their website's visibility on search engines. These services conduct thorough research to determine which keywords have high search volumes and low competition, providing businesses with valuable insights for their marketing strategies.

linkbuilding

A link building service is provided by an SEO agency that usually finds relevant websites that your target audience visits and places links back to your website. This is usually done by promoting your website content through manual outreach to as many relevant websites as possible.

Content creation

Is a professional service that helps businesses and individuals create high-quality content for their websites, blogs, social media platforms, and other marketing channels. These services typically include writing articles, blog posts, social media posts, website copy, product descriptions, and more.

On page SEO

the practice of optimizing webpages to improve a website's search engine rankings and earn organic traffic. In addition to publishing relevant, high-quality content, on-page SEO includes optimizing your headlines, HTML tags (title, meta, and header), and images.

What ranking factors do SEO services optimize for?

The ranking factors targeted by SEO services depend on the specifics of your chosen SEO plan. Full-service plans cover on-page, off-page, and technical SEO optimizations, while an off-page SEO plan will optimize off-page SEO. Google uses more than 200 ranking factors in its search algorithm.

Major ranking factors include

Your backlink profile

which is concerned with the quantity and quality of external sites linking to your site.

Your content

taking into account aspects like  keyword integration, content usefulness, and keyword targeting

User experience

which may involve elements such as mobile-friendliness, usability, page speed, among others

The exact ranking factors your SEO plan will optimize is contingent on your chosen SEO service provider.
