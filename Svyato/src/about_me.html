how

i started

Born in 2004 and raised in Riga (LV), I am based in Switzerland and study Computer Science at the ETH Zürich. I work as a freelance filmmaker and editor, while being very passionate about travel and landscape photography. My excitement for new technologies and developments in the film production space contribute significantly to bringing my ideas to the screens.

My filmmaking journey started with little travel films of a 15 year old, shot on a phone. Later, as I discovered my passion for travel and landscape photography, I found myself wanting to do motion picture more seriously. My first opportunity in this space was a short internship at Filmerei GmbH (Zürich, CH), where my first contacts in this space were made. From there, one project after another, I have been constantly improving and working on my filmmaking style.

hobby

In my free time, I do hiking and climbing - and when the weather decides to leave me in the city, you can often find me in rock climbing gyms. For the last decade, I’ve been a competitive Ballroom and Latin dancer, competing in international events.

I am very enthusiastic about creating captivating visuals and compelling, true-to-life stories without the overhead of a big production.
