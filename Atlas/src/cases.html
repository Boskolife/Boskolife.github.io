Case: Kroma.ai

Project overview

Kroma.ai, formerly known as SlideShop, offers an AI-based platform to create on-point pitches, proposals, and presentations that win. Presentations are brought to life through their data visualization tools, expert-created decks, and proprietary grab-and-go content. The company was still known as SlideShop when I first met with their leadership team. I partnered with this SaaS startup to support the transition from SlideShop to Kroma.ai, an AI-enabled presentation platform, and help them raise the second round of funding.

Project type

Brand communications Marketing strategy

Project skills

SEO, social media marketing Affiliate marketing, Google PPC

Technology stack

WordPress, SEMrush Klaviyo, Hootsuite
